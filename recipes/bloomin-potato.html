Bloomin Potato

Ingredients

  • 1 large russet potato
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chives, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)
  2. Scrub potato and prick with fork
  3. Bake for 45-50 minutes until tender
  4. Cut potato in half and scoop out center
  5. Mix scooped potato with butter, sour cream, and cheese
  6. Stuff mixture back into potato halves
  7. Bake for additional 10 minutes until golden
  8. Garnish with chives and serve
