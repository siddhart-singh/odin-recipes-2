Tiger Chicken

With the spiciness of carrot cake and the richness of cheesecake, these carrot cake cheesecake bars are the best of both worlds. These bars do need to chill several hours in the fridge before serving, so make sure to plan ahead.

  • 1 lb chicken leg quarters
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

    Steps

  1. Preheat the Instant Pot on the sauté setting.
  2. Add the olive oil and allow it to heat for a minute.
  3. Add the chicken and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the paprika, cumin, and garlic powder. Stir to combine.
  5. Press the "Cancel" button on the Instant Pot and secure the lid.
  6. Set the valve to "Sealing" and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
  7. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.
  8. Fluff the chicken with a fork and serve.
