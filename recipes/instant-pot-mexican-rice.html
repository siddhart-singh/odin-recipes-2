Instant Pot Mexican Rice

Description

Quick and easy Mexican rice made in the Instant Pot. Perfectly seasoned with a vibrant red color.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Set Instant Pot to sauté mode. Heat oil and cook onion until softened.
  2. Add garlic and rice, stirring for 2 minutes until rice is lightly toasted.
  3. Add tomato sauce, broth, cumin, and chili powder. Stir well.
  4. Close the lid and set to high pressure for 6 minutes.
  5. Allow natural release for 10 minutes, then quick release remaining pressure.
  6. Stir in peas and carrots. Let sit for 2 minutes.
  7. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
